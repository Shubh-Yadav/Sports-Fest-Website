Urja'17

All the students are hereby informed that the inter-house sports fest Urja'17 is going to be organized in the institute from 2nd November 2017 to 5th November 2017. Our annual inter-house sports festival is in line with our institute’s philosophy of augmenting sports persona to one’s technical excellence ensuring an all-round development of an individual. In the last few years, the undying efforts of the students and the support from the institute have helped us establish URJA as a brand name. URJA has captured eyes in its boundaries, but this year we aspire to make its presence felt wide over in the hearts of the students. We expect your kind support towards making this year’s festival an even greater success, establishing a benchmark of sports activities. We promise to put in our maximum efforts to achieve the goal.
